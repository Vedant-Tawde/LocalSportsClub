Welcome to NST FC

Founded in 1985, Newton FC has been the cornerstone of local football in our community for nearly four decades. We pride ourselves on developing young talent, promoting fair play, and bringing the community together through the beautiful game. Our club welcomes players of all skill levels and ages, from youth development programs to senior competitive teams.

OUR TEAM

Meet the dedicated players and coaching staff who make up Newton FC. Our team is a blend of experienced veterans and promising young talent, all committed to achieving excellence on and off the pitch. That's the power of friendship!!
