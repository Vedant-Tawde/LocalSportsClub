Welcome to NST SPORTS CLUB

Founded in 1985, NST SPORTS CLUB has been the cornerstone of local sports club in our community for nearly four decades. We pride ourselves on developing young talent, promoting fair play, and bringing the community together through the beautiful game. Our club welcomes players of all skill levels and ages, from youth development programs to senior competitive teams.

SERVICES

At NST SPORTS CLUB, we offer a range of services to support our players and the community we're dedicated to nurturing talent and promoting sportsmanship. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, there’s always a place for you here.
